Empathize

In this second part of the Design Thinking methodology we will cover the second objective:
Develop empathy with the users, by observing them. Therefore, it is a methodology based on observing the user.

Empathize

Therefore the problem "Failures in the cafeteria" was defined, it was visualized all the faults that it presents when selling a product and when attending to the public, to obtain concise data a proceeding was developed to develop a survey of thirty questions to understand how you feel about the cafeterias service.

EMPATHY TECHNIQUES

TECHNOLOGY OF WHAT ?, WHY? AND HOW?

In this part of this technique we carry out certain surveys like the following:

What?

• What do you suggest to make our product better?

• What do you suggest to avoid increasing insecurity on the streets?

• What would you buy to keep your community or your home safe?

• What is your problem with the environment of insecurity?

• What would you like our product to be nice for you?

How?

• How would you like it to be used?

• How would you arm it or would you need help from us?

• How would you use our product reliably or reliably?

Why?

• Why would our product use it?

• Why would I buy it if it is similar in the market?

• Why would I recommend it?

LISTEN TO UNDERSTAND, NOT TO ANSWER

In our case, we see that customers find our product in different brands, what they are worried about is the cost, the service and their benefits. Now we see what bothers our client that sometimes our part of alarm would not work since it can be manipulated by others.
